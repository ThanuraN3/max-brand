Maxbrands International is a specialist distributor of branded excess inventory and fast-moving consumer goods both throughout the Middle East and worldwide. Our close and long-lasting relationships with leading manufacturers and brand owners across the globe enables source and supply products at competitive prices

Maxbrands can purchase inventory which is considered to be slow moving or non-performing. We re-distribute these items into alternative

independent channels. Our customer base reaches out to a completely different demographic so that no conflict will occur with your current supply chain, both streams can co-exist harmoniously which actually provides a dual benefit.
